My Personal Rating

Out of all of the Star Wars films this is my favorite because of how emotional and epic it is. Revenge of the Sith (ROTS) has the best intro of any Star Wars movie. The only thing I don't like about this movie is the romantic dialog. Other than that, this movie is the best.
